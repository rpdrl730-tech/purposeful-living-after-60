Why this exists

Written for this chapter — not the last one

Most advice for people over 60 is either sugar-coated or scary. Purposeful Living After 60 is neither. It's honest, encouraging, and rooted in the real question that matters at this stage: how do I keep living with energy, security, and meaning?

"Purpose doesn't retire. It just changes shape."

Every article is practical enough to act on this week, and warm enough to make you feel seen. No jargon, no pressure — just a friend a few steps ahead, sharing what actually works.

And it's written for everyone in this chapter — every background, every story, every kind of second act. However your life looks, there's a place for you here.
