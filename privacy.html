Privacy Policy & Disclosures

Last updated: July 2026

Purposeful Living After 60 ("we", "us", "our") runs the website at purposefullivingafter60.com. We care about your privacy and aim to explain, in plain language, what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have. If anything here is unclear, email us at contact@purposefullivingafter60.com.

What information we collect

  • Information you give us. When you sign up for our newsletter, you provide your email address. When you use our contact form or email us, you share your name, email, and whatever you write to us.
  • Information collected automatically. Like most websites, we (and our service providers) may automatically collect standard technical information such as your browser type, device, approximate location, the pages you visit, and how you arrived at our site. This is gathered through cookies and similar technologies.

Cookies and analytics

Cookies are small text files stored on your device that help websites work and help owners understand how their site is used. We use them to keep the site functioning and to measure traffic through analytics tools (for example, a web analytics service that reports visitor numbers and popular pages in aggregate). This helps us understand which articles are useful so we can write more of what you value.

You can control or delete cookies through your browser settings, and you can set your browser to refuse most cookies. Some parts of the site may not work as smoothly if you do.

Advertising

We plan to display advertising on this site to help keep our content free. When advertising is active, third-party vendors — including Google, through the Google AdSense programme — may use cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to this and other websites. Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to show ads to you based on your visits to our site and/or other sites on the internet.

You may opt out of personalised advertising by visiting Google's Ads Settings, and you can learn more about how Google uses information from sites that use its services at policies.google.com/technologies/partner-sites. Advertising partners have their own privacy policies governing their use of cookies.

Affiliate disclosure

Some links on this site are "affiliate links." If you click one and buy something, we may earn a small commission — at no additional cost to you. We only recommend products and resources we genuinely believe may help our readers, and a commission never changes our honest opinion. Any article that contains affiliate links carries a clear note near the top. This disclosure is provided in the spirit of transparency and in line with advertising and consumer-protection guidelines.

How we use your information

  • To send you our newsletter and other emails you've asked for.
  • To respond to your messages and questions.
  • To operate, maintain, and improve the website and our content.
  • To understand aggregate traffic and how the site is used.
  • To comply with legal obligations and protect against misuse.

Third parties we share with

We do not sell your personal information. We share limited information only with service providers who help us run the site, such as our email newsletter provider, our website analytics provider, and advertising partners as described above. These providers are permitted to use your information only to provide their services to us. We may also disclose information if required by law.

How long we keep it

We keep newsletter subscriptions until you unsubscribe, and contact messages for as long as needed to respond and keep reasonable records. You can ask us to delete your information at any time.

Your rights and choices

  • Unsubscribe from our newsletter at any time using the link in every email.
  • Access, correct, or delete the personal information we hold about you — just email us.
  • Object to or restrict certain processing, where the law provides that right (for example, under the GDPR in Europe or the CCPA in California).
  • Control cookies through your browser and opt out of personalised ads as described above.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at contact@purposefullivingafter60.com.

Children

This site is intended for adults. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Not professional advice

The content on this site is for general information and education only. It is not medical, financial, or legal advice, and it is not a substitute for consulting a qualified professional about your own situation.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. When we do, we'll revise the "last updated" date above. Significant changes may also be announced on the site or by email.

Contact us
